III— The House

Twenty parcels.
Twenty thousand details.

Twenty Hampers began with a small, stubborn idea — that a corporate gift could be as considered as a personal letter, and that the act of giving deserved more time than the act of buying.

Plate II
Atelier interior — hands tying twine, paper rolls, stamps

We blend creativity, quality and thoughtful presentation into hampers that feel personally chosen — never assembled. Every parcel is curated with care, and every gesture is meant to be remembered long after the ribbon has been untied.

From elegant hand-block wraps and waxed-paper interleaves to the hand-stamped envelope your message arrives in — the smallest details are where the largest impressions are made.

We work in small batches, with named artisans, and refuse to ship anything we wouldn't be proud to receive ourselves. That has, to date, remained a useful filter.

Aanya & Kabir Mehra Founders
